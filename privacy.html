🔒 Plain English Summary

HVAC Unit Pro does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data whatsoever. All unit conversions happen instantly on your device. No account required. No internet connection required after the first load. No tracking. No ads. This app is a pure offline engineering tool.

1. About This App

HVAC Unit Pro is a free engineering unit conversion tool developed by hvacAI intel, operated by Utpal Joshi. The app is designed for HVAC engineers, MEP consultants, energy auditors, and facility managers to convert thermodynamic and engineering units across SI and IP systems.

The app is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA) and on the Google Play Store.

3. How the App Works

  • All unit conversion calculations are performed locally on your device using JavaScript.
  • No data is sent to any server at any time.
  • The app functions fully offline after the initial load via a service worker cache.
  • No login, registration, or account is required or offered.
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes.
  • Google Fonts are loaded on first use only, subject to Google's own privacy policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Fonts (Rajdhani, JetBrains Mono, Inter) for typography. When fonts are loaded, your device makes a standard HTTPS request to Google's font servers. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. After the first load, fonts are cached locally and Google is not contacted again.

No other third-party services, SDKs, or libraries are used.

5. Children's Privacy

HVAC Unit Pro is a professional engineering tool intended for adults. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, COPPA and equivalent regulations are satisfied by design.

6. Data Security

Because we collect no data, there is no personal data to secure, breach, or lose. Your unit conversion inputs never leave your device.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and equivalent regulations, you have rights regarding your personal data. Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, export, or delete. If you have any questions, contact us using the details below.
